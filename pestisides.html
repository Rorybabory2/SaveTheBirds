Pesticides are very bad for birds for many reasons. First is that when they are sprayed, they kill the bugs that many birds eat! Another reason is that some can harm the birds that breathe in the pesticides. Overall it is very important that we get rid of pesticides and instead use things like lady bugs to eat some of the aphids. One of the main pesticides is Roundup. However, Roundup is very bad for the birds and other life in the ecosystem. According to the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service 67 million birds die each year from pesticides. So don't use that.
